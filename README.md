# E-Commerce Customer Behavior Analysis

## End-to-End Customer Analytics using Python, PostgreSQL, SQL & Power BI

## Project Overview

Customer behavior analytics plays a critical role in enabling e-commerce organizations to understand purchasing patterns, improve customer engagement, and optimize business strategies. This project presents an end-to-end analytics workflow that transforms raw customer transaction data into actionable business insights through data preprocessing, database integration, SQL analysis, and interactive dashboard development.

The project demonstrates a complete analytics pipeline, emphasizing data quality, feature engineering, business intelligence, and visualization techniques commonly used in modern retail and product analytics environments.

## Business Problem

E-commerce businesses continuously collect large volumes of customer data; however, deriving meaningful insights from this information requires a structured analytical approach.

This project addresses the following business questions:

* Which customer segments contribute the highest revenue?
* How does customer purchasing behavior vary across demographics?
* What impact do subscription status and discounts have on customer engagement?
* Which product categories generate the highest sales and revenue?
* How can customer insights support strategic business decisions?

## Project Objectives

* Perform comprehensive data cleaning and preprocessing
* Conduct feature engineering for customer segmentation
* Integrate processed data into a PostgreSQL database
* Execute SQL-based business analysis
* Develop an interactive Power BI dashboard
* Generate business insights to support data-driven decision making

## Dashboard Features
The interactive dashboard provides comprehensive insights through:

* Total Customer Analysis
* Average Purchase Value
* Average Customer Review Rating
* Revenue Distribution by Product Category
* Sales Distribution by Product Category
* Revenue Analysis by Age Group
* Sales Analysis by Age Group
* Dynamic filtering based on Subscription Status, Gender, Product Category, and Shipping Type

## Key Business Insights

* Customer purchasing behavior varies significantly across demographic groups and product categories.
* Subscription status influences customer engagement and shopping frequency.
* Revenue contribution differs across age segments, enabling targeted marketing strategies.
* Interactive filtering supports deeper exploration of customer behavior and business performance metrics.
